FAQ

Questions, answered.

What kind of work can you automate?+

If it involves repetitive decisions, data movement, or language processing, it's a candidate. My experience is deepest in operations, sales, and marketing automation — but I always start by identifying where the biggest productivity gain is hiding.

How long until something is live?+

Most first automations are running in under two weeks. I start narrow, prove the value quickly, then expand from there.

Do we need a technical team?+

No. I handle everything from design to deployment to ongoing maintenance. Your team points me at the goal and reviews the results.
